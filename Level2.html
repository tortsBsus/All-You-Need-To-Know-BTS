BTS Related Shows!
Run BTS
Run BTS is a free to watch variety show where the agenda of each episode involves a game which all seven play, often against eachother, to win prizes and avoid punishments. What’s fascinating about Run BTS is not just its wholesome irreverence, but also the way it complements who BTS are as a group. It allows RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook to showcase totally different sides than what you see in their highly polished, carefully choreographed music videos and performances. They take whatever is thrown at them (and the producers throw a lot at them, like zombies) and good-naturedly make themselves look ridiculous for the entertainment of the viewers — and, perhaps best of all, themselves.
Bon Voyage
BTS: Bon Voyage is a reality show about BTS travelling around the world. With the first four seasons taking place in Norway, Hawaii, Malta and New Zealand, it shows a more laid back side of the guys.
In the Soop
“In the SOOP BTS Ver.” (“soop” means “forest” in Korean) So the show takes place in the forest. The concept is to spend time doing activities that are "somewhere between everyday life and relaxation."
Movies and Series
'Burn the Stage', 'Love Yourself in Seoul', 'Bring The Soul' and 'Break The Silence' are the few movies filmed during their tours which show the behind the scenes of the struggle of being a performer travelling all over the world.
