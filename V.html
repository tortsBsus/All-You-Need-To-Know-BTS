His own works


He stands out in the team for his vocals, dance moves and visuals.
But he also is making his own hits with collaborations
and sometimes with a little help from the leader RM.

He wrote and composed the song "4 o'Clock" with the help of RM.
• He states, "I wanted to perform a song that I made on stage,
a song that I wrote on my own and that song is 4 o'Clock."
You can also listen to the amazing rap and voice of RM blended along with V.

Taehyung shook the music industry with his first ever
self-composed song entitled "scenery" in Jan 2019. He said that when he
takes photos he sees them through the lens, the scenery, sky and streets.
he wanted to write what those things meant to him. They were not just
pictures, but a source of strength for him.

Taehyung also composed his very own English song for the first time in 2019
when he left his heartwarming single "Winter Bear" which is a well known
lullaby for all the fans.
On August 9th 2019, he even left a self-directed music video in which
he takes in the beauty of the world around him as he sings soulfully to army.

After Jimin's 'Christmas Love', V, on the same day, gifted the fans with
another holiday song "Snow Flower" which was
in collaboration with his well knownWooga squad member Peakboy.
