His works


Since he is the leader of the group, all the responsibility of the team comes down to him.
He works hard to compose, produce and write every song he releases.

"RM" is the debut mixtape by RM. It was released on March 20, 2015
with "Awakening" serving as the mixtape's title track.

Prior to the release, Rap Monster revealed the album art and the release date of "Awakening" music video
via Twitter. He ended the tweet with “U do u, I do I.” which is the catchphrase for this mixtape.
"RM" was listed in SPIN`s 50 Best Hip-Hop Album of 2015, later this year.

Mono (stylized as mono.) is the second mixtape and first playlist by Namjoon.
It was released on October 23, 2018 with "Forever Rain" serving as the mixtape's title track.
The mixtape topped the iTunes Top Albums Charts in a total of 92 countries and regions setting
the record with the most #1 spots on the chart by a Korean artist.

The track "Seoul" was produced by British electropop duo Honne.
He first discovered RM after seeing him recommend their music on Twitter and eventually met him in Seoul
following one of their concerts.The interaction caused the duo to want to collaborate with RM.

"Winter Flower" is a song by Younha featuring RM, and is the first track of her mini album Unstable Mindset.
The song was released on January 6, 2020.
